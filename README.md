US | Hello, how are you?
This ChatBot is only a simple version to execute the Conditional Branch technique, frequently used and referred to as "IF/ELSE".

This model does not feature AI integrations. However, below you will find a dictionary of questions that you can ask the ChatBot:

-"ola" | It will greet you
- "oi" | It will greet you
- "como voce esta" | It will show courtesy and ask you back
- "to bem" | It will continue the conversation
- "o que e python" | It will give you a brief explanation
- "tchau" | It will say goodbye

Have fun! :)
